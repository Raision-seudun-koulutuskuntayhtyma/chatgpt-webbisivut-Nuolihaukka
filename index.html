Bananas

Bananas are a popular fruit that are grown in tropical regions around the world. They are a good source of fiber, vitamin C, and potassium.

Types of Bananas

  • Cavendish bananas
  • Plantain bananas
  • Red bananas
  • Manzano bananas
  • Baby bananas

Benefits of Bananas

  • Helps to control blood pressure
  • Helps to reduce the risk of heart disease
  • Helps to reduce the risk of stroke
  • Helps to improve digestion

Recipes with Bananas

  1. Banana bread
  2. Banana smoothie
  3. Banana pudding
  4. Banana split

Fun Facts

Did you know that bananas are berries? They are also the world's most popular fruit!
